Q: What is Nandhiniketan at Srisailam and how to book it?
A: Nandhiniketan is the official guest house of Srisailam Devasthanam temple trust located very close to the main temple. It offers clean AC and non-AC rooms, cottages and dormitories at affordable rates. Rooms range from Rs 500 to Rs 2000 per night depending on type. It is the most recommended accommodation for pilgrims due to its proximity to the temple and trust management. Book online at srisailadevasthanam.org under the accommodation section. Book well in advance especially for weekends and festival seasons.

Q: What accommodation options are available at Srisailam?
A: Accommodation options at Srisailam from budget to comfort: Dharmashalas — free or Rs 50-100 per night, basic facilities, for budget pilgrims. Temple trust dormitories — Rs 100-300 per night, shared facilities. Nandhiniketan non-AC rooms — Rs 500-800 per night, clean, close to temple. Nandhiniketan AC rooms — Rs 1000-1500 per night, comfortable. Temple trust cottages — Rs 1500-2500 per night, good for families. Private hotels — Rs 800-3000 per night, various options near temple. Government guest houses — Rs 500-1000 per night, decent quality.

Q: How far is accommodation from Srisailam temple?
A: Distance of accommodations from Srisailam temple: Nandhiniketan — within temple premises, walking distance under 5 minutes. Temple trust cottages — 500 meters to 1 km from temple. Government guest houses — 1-2 km from temple. Private hotels in main town — 500 meters to 2 km from temple. Dharmashalas — within 1 km of temple. Most accommodation is walkable from the temple. Auto-rickshaws are available for Rs 20-50 for short distances within Srisailam town.

Q: How to book accommodation at Srisailam in advance?
A: Booking accommodation at Srisailam: Official temple trust accommodation (Nandhiniketan and cottages) — book at srisailadevasthanam.org under online booking section. Government accommodation — contact AP Tourism or local guest house directly. Private hotels — book through MakeMyTrip, Goibibo or directly call the hotel. For festival seasons like Maha Shivaratri and Karthika Masam book at least 2-3 months in advance. For regular weekends book 2-4 weeks ahead. Weekday bookings can usually be done 1 week ahead. Carry booking confirmation printout for check-in.

Q: What facilities are available in Srisailam town for pilgrims?
A: Facilities available in Srisailam for pilgrims: ATMs available near temple and in main market — carry cash as backup. Restaurants and dhabas serving South Indian vegetarian food throughout town. Medical facilities — government hospital and private clinics in Srisailam town. Pharmacies near temple. Temple Annadanam provides free meals daily — timing 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM approximately. Prasadam shops selling vibhuti, kumkum, bilva leaves, flowers near temple. Locker facilities near temple entrance for footwear and mobile phones. Cloakroom for luggage near bus stand.

Q: What is the cost of a complete Srisailam pilgrimage trip?
A: Estimated budget for Srisailam pilgrimage for a family of 4: Transport from Hyderabad by car (petrol) — Rs 2000-3000 round trip. Or APSRTC bus — Rs 800-1200 total for 4 people round trip. Accommodation for 2 nights — Rs 2000-6000 depending on choice. Meals — Rs 1000-2000 (temple Annadanam is free for one meal). Darshan tickets — Sarva Darshan is free. Sevas — Rs 500-5000 depending on seva chosen. Miscellaneous — Rs 500. Total budget trip — Rs 5000-8000 for family of 4. Comfortable trip — Rs 10000-15000 for family of 4.

Q: Are there free accommodation options at Srisailam?
A: Free accommodation at Srisailam: Several dharmashalas (pilgrim rest houses) offer free or nominal cost accommodation. These are typically managed by charitable trusts and community organizations. Facilities are basic — dormitory style with shared bathrooms. Blankets may not be provided — carry your own. Some dharmashalas require a small identity proof. During festival seasons these fill up extremely quickly. The temple Annadanam provides free food so pilgrims on very tight budgets can manage with minimal cost.

Q: What are private hotel options near Srisailam temple?
A: Private hotels near Srisailam temple: Several lodges and hotels are located in Srisailam town within 1-2 km of the temple. Most offer basic to comfortable rooms ranging from Rs 800 to Rs 3000 per night. Popular options include hotels on the main road leading to temple. All provide vegetarian meals or have restaurants nearby. Check-in is typically after 12 PM and check-out by 11 AM. Most accept UPI payments. Call ahead during festival seasons to confirm availability as they fill up fast.
