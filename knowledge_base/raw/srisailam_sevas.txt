Srisailam Temple Sevas Information
Temple: Sri Bhramaramba Mallikarjuna Swamy Temple
Location: Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh, India

----------------------------------------
SEVAS OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------
The temple offers various sevas (ritual services) that devotees can participate in. These sevas are categorized into daily sevas, arjitha sevas (paid), and special/festival sevas.

----------------------------------------
DAILY SEVAS
----------------------------------------

1. Suprabhata Seva
- Time: Early morning (~4:30 AM)
- Description: Waking the deity with Vedic chants.

2. Rudrabhishekam
- Time: Morning and evening
- Description: Abhishekam to Lord Mallikarjuna using sacred items.
- Importance: Removes negative karma and brings spiritual purification.

3. Panchamruta Abhishekam
- Description: Ritual bath using milk, curd, ghee, honey, and sugar.

4. Ashtottara Archana
- Description: Chanting 108 names of Lord Shiva with offerings.

5. Kumkum Archana
- Performed for: Goddess Bhramaramba
- Purpose: Prosperity and family well-being.

6. Harathi Sevas
- Includes:
  - Morning Harathi
  - Afternoon Harathi
  - Evening Harathi

----------------------------------------
ARJITHA SEVAS (PAID SEVAS)
----------------------------------------

1. Sparsha Darshan
- Description: Devotees can touch the Shiva Lingam.
- Note: Limited slots available.

2. Abhishekam Seva
- Includes:
  - Rudrabhishekam
  - Panchamruta Abhishekam
- Booking: Online or temple counter.

3. Kalyanotsavam
- Description: Divine marriage of Lord Mallikarjuna and Goddess Bhramaramba.
- Benefit: Harmony in married life.

4. Pallaki Seva
- Time: Evening
- Description: Procession of the deity in a palanquin.

5. Sahasranamarchana
- Description: Chanting 1000 names of the deity.

6. Ekadasa Rudrabhishekam
- Description: Advanced ritual with 11 recitations of Rudram.

----------------------------------------
SPECIAL SEVAS
----------------------------------------

1. Maha Rudrabhishekam
- Performed during: Maha Shivaratri
- Significance: Highly auspicious and powerful.

2. Laksha Bilwarchana
- Offering of 100,000 bilva leaves.
- Performed during: Karthika Masam.

3. Chandi Homam
- Performed during: Navratri
- Purpose: Protection and prosperity.

4. Shani Trayodashi Seva
- Special abhishekam on Shani Trayodashi days.

----------------------------------------
BOOKING INFORMATION
----------------------------------------

- Sevas can be booked through:
  1. Official temple website
  2. Temple counters at Srisailam

- Advance booking is recommended for:
  - Kalyanotsavam
  - Sparsha Darshan
  - Festival sevas

----------------------------------------
GENERAL GUIDELINES
----------------------------------------

- Devotees must follow dress code:
  - Traditional attire preferred
- Carry valid ID proof for booked sevas
- Report at least 30–60 minutes before seva time
- Mobile phones may be restricted inside temple premises

----------------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTES
----------------------------------------

- Seva timings may change during festivals
- Tickets are subject to availability
- Refund policies depend on temple rules
