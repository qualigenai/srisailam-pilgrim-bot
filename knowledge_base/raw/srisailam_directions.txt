SECTION 2: NEAREST AIRPORTS — FULL DETAIL
==============================================================

AIRPORT 1: RAJIV GANDHI INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT (HYD)
  Location     : Shamshabad, Hyderabad, Telangana
  Distance     : ~196–217 km from Srisailam
  Drive time   : ~4–5 hours by road
  Connections  : Well connected to Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai,
                 Bangalore, Goa, Jaipur, Kochi, Coimbatore, Pune,
                 Ahmedabad, Bhubaneswar, and many international cities.
  Airlines     : IndiGo, Air India, SpiceJet, Akasa Air, Vistara,
                 and others.
  From airport to Srisailam:
    Option A — Cab/taxi: Hire a prepaid cab or app-based cab
               (OLA/Uber) from the airport directly to Srisailam.
               Fare: ₹2,500–₹4,500 (depending on vehicle type).
               Journey: ~4–5 hours.
    Option B — Bus: Take a cab to Hyderabad MGBS (Mahatma Gandhi
               Bus Station / Koti / Jubilee Bus Station), then board
               an APSRTC or TSRTC bus to Srisailam. Journey: ~7 hrs.
    Option C — Cab to Hyderabad city, then direct bus from MGBS/JBS.
  Tip: Booking a direct cab from the airport is the most comfortable
       option for pilgrims arriving with family or luggage.

SECTION 3: NEAREST RAILWAY STATIONS — FULL DETAIL
==============================================================

NOTE: Srisailam has NO railway station. The town is not yet
connected by rail directly. All rail routes require a final
road segment (taxi, bus, or auto-rickshaw) from the nearest
railhead.

RAILWAY STATION 1: MARKAPUR ROAD (Code: MRK) — PRIMARY CHOICE
  Distance     : ~81–90 km from Srisailam
  Drive time   : ~2–2.5 hours (hilly roads)
  Zone         : South Central Railway, Guntur Division
  Connectivity : Hyderabad (Kacheguda/Secunderabad), Vijayawada,
                 Guntur, Chennai, Bangalore (some trains)
  Key trains   :
    - KCG GNT Express (17251) — Kacheguda to Guntur
    - Kacheguda Express
    - Tungabhadra Express
    - Amaravathi Express
    - Howrah Express (via Secunderabad)
    - Prasanthi Express
    - HWH SSPN Express
  Onward to Srisailam:
    - Taxi (hire from outside station): ₹1,200–₹2,000 | ~2 hrs
    - APSRTC bus from Markapur to Srisailam: Cheaper option
    - Auto-rickshaw to bus stand, then bus to Srisailam
  Tip: Markapur Road is the single most recommended railhead for
       Srisailam. Book in advance during Maha Shivaratri.

RAILWAY STATION 2: CUMBUM (Code: CMB)
  Distance     : ~70 km from Srisailam (closer, but fewer trains)
  Drive time   : ~1.5–2 hours
  Connectivity : Limited trains; check availability on IRCTC.
                 Some Kondaveedu Express / Prasanthi Express stop here.
  Onward to Srisailam:
    - Taxi: ₹1,000–₹1,500 | ~1.5 hrs
    - Few local buses available; taxi is more reliable

RAILWAY STATION 3: NANDYAL (Code: NDL) — MAJOR JUNCTION
  Distance     : ~132–140 km from Srisailam
  Drive time   : ~3 hours
  Connectivity : Good junction. Trains from Hyderabad, Bangalore,
                 Guntur, and Secunderabad stop here.
  Key trains   :
    - Rayalaseema Express
    - Kacheguda-Yesvantpur Express
    - Bangalore-Hyderabad Express
  Onward to Srisailam:
    - Taxi: ₹1,800–₹2,500 | ~3 hrs
    - APSRTC bus from Nandyal to Srisailam
  Tip: Nandyal is a good choice for travelers from Bangalore/
       Hyderabad who do not get a train to Markapur.

RAILWAY STATION 4: KURNOOL CITY (Code: KRNT)
  Distance     : ~180 km from Srisailam
  Drive time   : ~4–5 hours
  Connectivity : Well-connected to Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai.
                 Regular trains from major cities of South India.
  Key trains   :
    - Kacheguda Express (from Chennai Egmore)
    - Trains from Bangalore and Hyderabad
  Onward to Srisailam:
    Route: Kurnool → Nandikotkur → Atmakur → Dornala → Srisailam
    - APSRTC bus (express buses every hour from 5 AM to 11:30 PM)
    - Taxi: ₹2,500–₹3,500 | ~4–5 hrs
  Tip: Kurnool is a major transport hub. Good alternative if no
       direct train/bus to Markapur is available.

RAILWAY STATION 5: TARLUPADU
  Distance     : ~100 km from Srisailam
  Drive time   : ~2.5 hours
  Note         : Small station with limited trains. Good for
                 travelers with flexible schedules. Taxi available.

RAILWAY STATION 6: JAGGAMBHOTLA KRISHNAPURAM
  Distance     : ~95 km from Srisailam
  Note         : Small, limited trains. Offbeat option. Taxis
                 available especially during festive seasons.

RAILWAY STATION 7: HYDERABAD / SECUNDERABAD / KACHEGUDA
  Distance     : ~213–231 km from Srisailam
  Note         : For long-distance travelers, Hyderabad is the

  Key hub      : MGBS (Mahatma Gandhi Bus Station), also called

SECTION 5: BY ROAD — PRIVATE VEHICLE & SELF-DRIVE
==============================================================

NOTE: Road access is the ONLY direct way to reach Srisailam.
The last stretch (typically the Nallamala forest ghat section)
is scenic but winding — with hairpin bends, steep turns,
forest gates, and limited lighting. Night driving on ghat
stretches is strongly discouraged.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ROUTE 1: HYDERABAD → SRISAILAM (Primary route)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Total distance  : ~213 km
  Total time      : ~4–5 hrs (car), ~5–7 hrs (bus)
  National Highway: NH 765 (primary route)

  ROUTE A (via Nallamala forest — most scenic):
    Hyderabad → ORR (Outer Ring Road) → Shadnagar → Jadcherla →
    Mahbubnagar → Mannanur → Nallamala Forest Checkpoint →

    Atmakur → Dornala → Srisailam

  Fuel/toll notes:

    - Fewer fuel stations once inside forest.
    - Carry FASTag or cash for tolls on NH765.

    - Dhabas at Miryalaguda / Jadcherla area

SECTION 6: LOCAL TRANSPORT WITHIN SRISAILAM
==============================================================

  Auto-rickshaws : Available in Srisailam town for short distances.
  Share autos    : Run between temple, bus stand, and dam.
  APSRTC local   : Local buses to Patala Ganga, Sakshi Ganapathi,
                   Sikharam (ropeway point).
  Ropeway        : Cable car connects Srisailam plateau to the
                   Patala Ganga (Krishna River) level and also
                   to Akka Mahadevi Caves (across the river).
  Boat rides     : Available at Krishna River (Patala Ganga) for
                   Akka Mahadevi Caves and river sightseeing.
  Forest jeep    : For Mallela Theertham waterfall (~58 km away),
                   hire a terrain vehicle from Srisailam.
                   Cost: ~₹300/person (shared) or ₹1,200 for group of 10.
  Temple shuttle : Devasthanam manages transport within the complex.

SECTION 7: TRAVEL TIPS FOR ALL VISITORS
==============================================================

TIMING & SEASON:
  - Best season   : October – February (cool, pleasant)
  - Avoid         : Peak monsoon (July–August); ghat roads become
                    slippery; river may be in flood.
  - Summer        : March–June is hot in the plains; temple plateau
                    is slightly cooler.
  - Festival peaks: Maha Shivaratri, Brahmotsavam, Karthika Masam —
                    book transport and accommodation months in advance.

ROAD SAFETY — GHAT SECTION:
  - The last 25–30 km before Srisailam through Nallamala forest
    is a narrow, winding ghat road with hairpin bends.
  - NEVER drive this section at night.
  - Trucks and heavy vehicles share the road; drive slowly.
  - Watch for wildlife crossing (this is a Tiger Reserve).
  - Carry spare tyre, basic toolkit, first-aid kit.
  - Fuel up before entering the forest (limited petrol bunks).

FOREST CHECKPOINT:
  - Entry into Nallamala forest is restricted after sunset and
    before sunrise. Carry valid government ID.
  - Forest gates open: approximately 6 AM – 6 PM (verify locally).
  - Two-wheelers: Check current forest department permissions
    before entering the Nallamala stretch on a bike.

MONEY & CONNECTIVITY:
  - Carry adequate CASH — ATMs in Srisailam town are limited.
  - Mobile signal can be weak or intermittent in forest/ghat areas
    and near the river base (Patala Ganga).
  - Keep downloaded offline maps (Google Maps / Maps.me) as backup.

BOOKING IN ADVANCE:
  - Bus tickets: APSRTC online portal, Redbus, Abhibus.
  - Train tickets: IRCTC (irctc.co.in). Book well in advance
    for Markapur Road station during festival season.
  - Accommodation: srisailadevasthanam.org (Devasthanam rooms),
    Haritha Hotel (TTDC), Punnami Srisailam (TTDC), private hotels.
  - Sevas & darshan: srisailadevasthanam.org (online booking).

WHAT TO CARRY:
  - Government ID (mandatory for forest entry, temple special darshan)
  - Cash (₹2,000–₹5,000 minimum)
  - Comfortable footwear (many steps: 852 steps to Patala Ganga)
  - Modest/traditional clothing (dhotis, sarees, salwar kameez)
  - Bottled water and light snacks for the road
  - Offline navigation app (especially for forest section)
  - FASTag for highway tolls

DRESS CODE AT TEMPLE:
  - Men     : Dhoti, lungi, or full-length trousers with shirt
  - Women   : Saree, salwar kameez, or any modest attire
  - Shorts, sleeveless tops, and revealing clothing NOT permitted

PHOTOGRAPHY:
  - NOT allowed inside the sanctum of the main temple.
  - Allowed in outer areas, gardens, dam viewpoint, and forest.

FOOD:
  - Srisailam is a temple town — only vegetarian food is available.
  - Temple Annadanam (free meals) available daily.
  - Good restaurants along the main road near the temple.
  - Do not rely on roadside food between Hyderabad and Srisailam;
    carry snacks or eat at major dhaba stops.

SECTION 9: RECOMMENDED ITINERARY APPROACH BY ORIGIN
==============================================================

FROM HYDERABAD (same day or overnight):
  Day 1: Depart MGBS 05:30 AM → Arrive Srisailam ~10:30 AM →
         Temple darshan → Patala Ganga → Sakshi Ganapathi →
         Return bus 05:00 PM → Reach Hyderabad ~11:00 PM.
  Stay: Opt for overnight to explore comfortably at leisure.

FROM BANGALORE (2 nights recommended):
  Day 0 (evening): Board overnight APSRTC bus from Majestic.
  Day 1 (morning): Arrive Srisailam → Darshan → Sightseeing.
  Day 2 (morning): Patala Ganga, Akka Mahadevi Caves (ropeway+boat).
  Day 2 (evening): Return overnight bus to Bangalore.

FROM CHENNAI (2 nights):
  Day 0: Train Chennai Egmore → Kurnool (Kacheguda Express).
  Day 1 AM: Taxi/bus Kurnool → Srisailam. Darshan.
  Day 2: Sightseeing. Return journey via Kurnool → Chennai by train.

FROM DELHI / MUMBAI / OTHER DISTANT CITIES:
  Day 1: Fly to Hyderabad. Check in at Hyderabad hotel.
  Day 2 AM: Private cab Hyderabad → Srisailam. Darshan.
  Day 2-3: Stay in Srisailam (book Devasthanam rooms in advance).
  Day 3-4: Return to Hyderabad → fly home.
